Bio

I am now an Associate Professor at Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ISCAS), and work in the Technique Center of Software Engineering (TCSE) and the State Key Laboratory of Computer Science (SKLCS) at ISCAS. I got my Ph.D. degree in January 2014 in Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences. I received my Master degree in Computer Science from University of Electronic Sciences and Technology of China in 2008.
Research interests:
My research research interests include Software Reliability Engineering (SRE), Artificial Intelligencefor IT Operations (AIops), Cloud Computing, Service Computing and now with a focus on Optimization for Big Data.
